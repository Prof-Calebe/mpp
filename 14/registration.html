Registration


The Marathon will be open to teams of up to three participants, who must be undergraduate or graduate students. All of them must be registered to the WSCAD conference (see the special fee). A substitute member can be indicated, and one substitution will be allowed during the marathon.


Registration is closed.

Remember that the team members must be registered at WSCAD Conference to participate in the contest.
