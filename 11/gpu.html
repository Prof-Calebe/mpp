2nd GPU Programming Contest


In 2011, the Marathon of Parallel Programming hosts the 2nd GPU Special Programming Contest.

The same rules are applied on this contest. The winners will be officially announced during the special meeting of 23nd SBAC-PAD committee.

Awards

The awards will be offered by the sponsors companies:

  • 1 GeForce GTX 550Ti
  • 1 GeForce GT 520
  • 1 GeForce GT 430

Program

The GPU Contest will be hosted at:

October 27th, 2011 (2pm): Contest


In conjunction with International Symposium on Computer Architecture and High Performance Computing - SBAC-PAD 2011, in Vitória, ES, Brazil.

Winners

This year, there is no regular winner. But, special honor are given to these teams:

  • Two and a half men (UFV)
    • Chaulio de Resende Ferreira, Oberlan Christo Romão and Raphael Rodrigues Campos
  • SDK (UFRGS)
    • João Vicente Ferreira Lima, Vinicius Garcia Pinto and Silvio Ricardo Cordeiro
  • UFES
    • Jorcy de Oliveira Neto, Lucas de Paula Veronese and Lucas Catabriga Rocha

The Problems

The problem set used in the GPU contest is:

  • Problems (some inputs)
    1. Leibniz's PI - sequential
    2. Mandelbrot - sequential
    3. Permutation Flowshop Scheduling - sequential
    4. Minimum Spanning Tree - sequential
