Registration


The Marathon will be open to teams of up to three participants, who must be undergraduate or graduate students. All of them must be registered to the SBAC-PAD conference (see the special fee). A substitute member can be indicated, and one substitution will be allowed during the marathon.

Registration is closed.

Remember that the team members must be registered at SBAC-PAD 2011 Conference to participate the contest.
